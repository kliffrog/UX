Author

User Experience Design

According to Wikipedia, user experience design (UX, UXD, UED or XD) is "the process of enhancing user satisfaction by improving the usability, accessibility, and pleasure provided in the interaction between the user and the product."

End users are the future of your company. Being able to give them a product with the features and capabilities they need will keep them coming back for more.

User experience design is being able to see your product through your customers eyes. You can see what features they use the most and which ones they ignore. Then you can focus your team's energy on the parts of your product that your public wants.
